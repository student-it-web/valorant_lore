VALORANT LORE .

WHAT MAKES VALORANT INTERESTING


Valorant is a high-stakes multiversal thriller where two parallel Earths fight for survival. Instead of clear villains, the lore features the VALORANT Protocol defending their world from "mirror" versions of themselves in a desperate battle over reality-bending resources.

AGENTS STORY

EACH OF THEM HAS DIFFERENT EXPERIENCES


From ancient monks and outlaws to tech-geniuses and ghosts, the Valorant Protocol is a global collision of stories. Whether forged in supernatural power or raw grit, these agents have traded mysterious pasts for a shared future. It’s not just a squad—it’s a volatile mix of legends fighting for a world as broken as they are.

YOUR MAPS

EXPLORE THE WORLD


VALORANT maps are interdimensional crime scenes where architectural scars and abandoned tech reveal a desperate war for Radianite. More than just tactical arenas, these locations serve as physical evidence of a geopolitical struggle between two parallel worlds fighting for survival.
