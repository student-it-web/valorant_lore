    MAPS

    VALORANT

    ABYSS

    Deep within the frozen chasm of Jan Mayen, Norway, lies the Abyss, a clandestine fortress belonging to the ancient and shadow-dwelling Scions of the Hourglass. Far from the corporate greed of Kingdom Corp, this "Elder Grotto" serves as a sanctuary and a prison where the world’s most dangerous secrets are "pruned" from history. It is here that the Scions have spent centuries suppressing the truth about Radianite, going so far as to contain powerful beings like Vyse within its metallic, thorn-choked corridors. The map acts as a graveyard for lost knowledge, where the echoes of Omen’s forgotten past as an assassin collide with the terrifying realization that some truths were never meant to reach the surface.

    SUNSET

    Located in the Boyle Heights neighborhood of East Los Angeles, Sunset is a battlefield defined by the collision between tight-knit community resilience and the intrusive expansion of the Kingdom Corporation. The map centers on a secretive Kingdom facility that sparked local outrage before a catastrophic failure in an underground portal created a massive, glowing sinkhole that devastated the surrounding commercial district. For agents like Gekko, who calls this neighborhood home, the fight on Sunset is deeply personal, representing a struggle to protect his heritage from the fallout of corporate experiments that left the streets of LA illuminated by a permanent, eerie Radianite glow.

    PEARL

    Set in a vibrant, underwater version of Lisbon, Portugal, Pearl is the first map located on Omega Earth, a mirror dimension struggling with extreme climate change and rising sea levels. To save the historic city from being swallowed by the Atlantic, the VALORANT Legion and Kingdom Industries constructed a massive, Radianite-powered geo-dome that preserves a portion of the metropolis beneath the waves. While the city appears as a thriving, high-tech sanctuary where agents are celebrated as public heroes with statues and museum exhibits, the map’s lore reveals a desperate survival story where the inhabitants must live under constant technological protection to escape their dying world's environmental collapse.

    LOTUS

    Hidden within the lush Western Ghats of India on Omega Earth, Lotus—known as the "City of Flowers"—is a sprawling complex of ancient ruins built by the mysterious "Guardians" in collaboration with humanity. Unlike the high-tech industrial sites of Kingdom Corp, this mystical metropolis is powered by "root circuitry"—massive, Radianite-infused plants and giant lotus flowers that channel solar energy to operate the site’s legendary rotating stone doors and heavy mechanisms. As a multiverse nexus, Lotus holds a powerful connection to the astral realm, leading agents like Harbor and Astra to protect it from those who would exploit its ancient technology and risk a catastrophic collapse that could threaten millions across both Alpha and Omega Earth.
